Discover the Difference: Why Our B.Ed Program Stands Out

Choose us for a legacy of excellence in teacher education, where you'll be mentored by distinguished educators. Immerse yourself in a learning environment that's as dynamic as it is rich in history.

Experienced and Dedicated Faculty

Our greatest strength lies in our faculty. Comprising highly qualified and experienced educators, our team is dedicated to providing an enriching learning experience.

Our professors and lecturers bring a wealth of knowledge and real-world expertise, ensuring that our B.Ed program is not just academically rigorous but also relevant to the current educational landscape.

A Legacy of Educational Excellence

Established in 1956, our college has a distinguished history as one of the oldest institutions specializing in teacher education. Over the decades, we have built a legacy of excellence, contributing significantly to the field of education.

This rich history and experience translate into a deep understanding of the evolving needs of educators and the education sector.

State-of-the-Art Learning Environment

Our commitment to providing a top-tier education is reflected in our state-of-the-art facilities and resources. From advanced technology in classrooms to extensive libraries and modern teaching aids, we ensure that our students have access to the best learning tools.

These facilities, combined with our dynamic campus culture and supportive community, create an ideal environment for aspiring educators to thrive and develop the skills needed for a successful career in teaching.
